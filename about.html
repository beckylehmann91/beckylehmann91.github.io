Hello! Here to learn a little more about me?

My name is Becky Lehmann, I graduated from Miami University in 2014 with a degree in Marketing. For the past year and a half, worked for Allstate Insurance Company as a member of the Leadership Development Program. I love problem solving and a good challenge. I'm a fan of the outdoors and anything active (break a good sweat), and can be found frequenting the Lincoln Park Zoo. I believe in laughter, silliness, curiosity and preserving your inner child. My life mission is to constantly learn from and inspire others, to create beautiful things and to live the adventure.
